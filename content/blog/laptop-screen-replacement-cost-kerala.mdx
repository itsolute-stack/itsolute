If you've cracked your laptop screen, the only useful question is: what will it cost to fix?

Here are real 2026 prices from our Parthas Lane workshop. We service screens for every major laptop brand in Kerala. These numbers are honest — neither the cheapest nor the most expensive in the market, but what genuine work actually costs.

## Standard 14" or 15.6" laptop screens

For HP, Dell, Lenovo, Asus, Acer business and consumer laptops with standard FHD displays:

| Screen type | Genuine OEM | Compatible (Grade A) |
|---|---|---|
| HD (1366×768) — older budget laptops | ₹3,200-4,000 | ₹2,400-3,000 |
| FHD (1920×1080) IPS non-touch | ₹4,500-6,500 | ₹3,200-4,500 |
| FHD touchscreen | ₹8,500-12,000 | ₹6,000-8,500 |
| 2K / QHD (2560×1440) | ₹10,000-15,000 | ₹7,000-10,000 |

Add ₹500-800 for labour. Most replacements take 60-90 minutes.

**Genuine OEM panels** come from the same factories that made the original — LG, BOE, AUO, Samsung. Identical to what shipped with your laptop. Slightly more expensive, longer life expectancy.

**Compatible Grade A panels** are panels from those same manufacturers, different product line — same spec, same fit, same connector, same image quality. The difference is factory binning. For general office and personal use, the daily-life difference is undetectable.

For 90% of users, compatible Grade A is the right choice. ₹1,500-3,000 saved with no daily compromise. We use Grade A compatibles by default and offer genuine OEM as an upgrade.

## MacBook screen replacement

MacBook screens are a different conversation. Apple sells the screen as a "display assembly" bonded with the lid, hinge, antenna, and front camera — you can't replace just the panel.

| MacBook model | Assembly replacement |
|---|---|
| MacBook Air M1 / M2 13" | ₹28,000-34,000 |
| MacBook Air M2 / M3 15" | ₹38,000-45,000 |
| MacBook Pro 13" (Intel, recent) | ₹32,000-40,000 |
| MacBook Pro 14" (M1/M2/M3 Pro) | ₹45,000-58,000 |
| MacBook Pro 16" (M1/M2/M3 Pro/Max) | ₹62,000-78,000 |
| Older MacBook Pro Retina (Intel) | ₹22,000-32,000 |

Real Kerala market prices for genuine Apple display assemblies through authorised channels. The Apple Service Centre often charges 20-40% more — partly bundled labour and Apple overhead, partly because they sometimes recommend whole-unit swap.

For older Intel MacBook Pros (2018-2020), independent shops can sometimes source panel-only replacements at ₹12,000-18,000. We can do this work but discuss the tradeoffs honestly: shorter warranty on panel alone, and the bonded assembly is harder to refit cleanly.

## What affects final cost

Beyond the panel:

**Hinge damage.** If dropped, hinges might be cracked too. Hinge replacement adds ₹800-1,500.
**Bezel damage.** Plastic frame around the screen sometimes cracks too. ₹400-800.
**LCD cable damage.** The cable through the hinge can fail after repeated stress. ₹600-1,200.
**Webcam integration.** Some modern panels have the webcam bonded — panel replacement includes a new webcam.
**Anti-glare film.** Some business laptops have matte/anti-glare film. Mention this when quoting — different part.
**Touch digitiser separation.** On touchscreens, sometimes only the glass breaks but the LCD is fine. Theoretically you can replace just the digitiser — but labour cost (de-bond, re-bond) usually exceeds the panel-replacement cost.

## Service centre vs independent workshop

**Authorised service centres** are right when your laptop is under manufacturer warranty, you want OEM parts no questions asked, you're not in a hurry (turnaround often 5-15 working days), or repair is covered by accidental damage protection.

**Independent workshops** (us included) are right when warranty has expired, you need same-day or next-day turnaround, cost matters more than brand exclusivity, or the laptop is older or out of OEM service network.

For a 3-year-old Dell Vostro needing a screen swap, the choice is obvious. For a 6-month-old ThinkPad still under warranty, take it to Lenovo.

## What to do right now

If your laptop screen is cracked or damaged:

1. Don't keep using it. Pressure on a cracked LCD spreads damage.
2. Don't tape it or apply pressure. The crack worsens.
3. Power it down properly — repeated boots stress the connector.
